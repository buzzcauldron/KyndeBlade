QUICK REFERENCE: How to Place Characters
=========================================

EASIEST METHOD:
1. Press Shift+1 (opens Place Actors)
2. Search: "Test Level Setup"
3. Drag it into level
4. Press Play
Done!

CONSOLE COMMAND (if actor doesn't work):
1. Press Play
2. Press ` (backtick) to open console
3. Type: SpawnTestCharacters
4. Press Enter

MANUAL PLACEMENT:
1. Shift+1 → Search for "Knight Character"
2. Drag into level
3. Repeat for "Mage Character", "False Character", etc.
4. Position them manually

KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS:
- Shift+1 = Place Actors panel
- ` = Console (in Play mode)
- Alt+P = Play
- F = Focus on selected
