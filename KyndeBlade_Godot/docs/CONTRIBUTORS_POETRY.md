# Contributors’ poetry (community coauthorship, gated)

**Purpose:** Short **Middle English–modern** lines (or strictly modern lines *in voice*) that the lead **curates** into the game. You remain **author of the frame**; contributors are **credited by handle or name**.

## Rules (non-negotiable for merge)

1. **Length:** one line, **≤ 140 characters** (including spaces).
2. **Voice:** align with [`SOURCES_AND_CREDITS.md`](../../ProjectArchive/UnityKyndeBlade/KyndeBlade_Unity/Assets/KyndeBlade/Docs/SOURCES_AND_CREDITS.md) and spelling choices (*feeld*, *folke*, etc.) where applicable.
3. **Content:** no slurs; no gratuitous graphic harm; poverty/hunger themes require **care**—consult [`CULT_SHIP_BAR.md`](CULT_SHIP_BAR.md) playtest bar.
4. **License:** by submitting, contributor agrees their **submitted line(s)** are licensed to the project under **CC0 1.0** *or* signs a **one-line assignment** (use email trail or PR template text below).

## PR / submission template (paste in PR description)

